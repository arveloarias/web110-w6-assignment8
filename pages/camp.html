The Light On the Sky

Beautiful sky in the camp at night. Then a campfire. The moon in the sky looked like it was ready to look at me with the fire. The stars were out all over the campground. The campfire was lovely; they lit the skies with a bright flame. Ah, so beautiful. Nice camping.

Night Fire

This campfire at night was so warm and intimate. We found ourselves in the dark, sitting close and getting to know one another.

Campfire Sing

They were singing around the campfire with friends in a beautiful daydream, and I was laughing about a goofball joke from the day before.

Inclusion

Leaving

Leaving the camp, Fred waved as he walked along the road, knowing that Felix was going home. Leaving it was going to be hard, but he was glad Felix came to the camp.

Lucas Tea

Tea with Lucas at the camp, sitting on logs near the lake line. I had a small campfire that lasted long enough for me to drink most of a cup of tea before saying I had to go to the bathroom.
